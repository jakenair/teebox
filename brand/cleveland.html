Why Cleveland owns the wedge category

Cleveland Golf has been a wedge-first brand since Roger Cleveland — yes, that Roger Cleveland — designed the original 588 in the 1980s. Forty years and several ownership changes later, Cleveland is still where most players who care about wedges go first. The RTX 6 ZipCore is one of the cleanest tour-shape wedges in golf, with grooves that grip aggressively and a head shape that just looks right at address.

But Cleveland isn't only wedges. The CBX line is the answer for players who want wedge versatility without going to a true blade — wider sole, more forgiveness, same RTX face technology. The HB Soft putters are some of the most reasonably-priced legitimate putters on the market. And the Frontline series is a quietly excellent putter line.

On TeeBox, Cleveland wedges show up constantly — players cycle them every season as grooves wear. CBX 4 ZipCore, RTX 6 ZipCore, even older 588s in great shape. Real photos, direct messaging, no listing fees.

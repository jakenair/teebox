Our Mission

Good gear deserves a second round.

TeeBox is the peer-to-peer marketplace built for golfers — a trusted place to buy and sell clubs, apparel, and accessories directly between members.

Who we are

We're golfers who were tired of two bad options for used gear: lowball trade-in counters, or sprawling general marketplaces where nobody knows a blade from a cavity-back. So we built something in between — a marketplace just for golf, where the people on both sides of the deal actually play.

Every listing is a real club, bag, or piece of kit from a fellow member. No retailers, no drop-shippers, no markup for being in the middle.

The mission

Keep great equipment in play, and make buying and selling it feel fair and safe. The driver you've outgrown is someone else's breakthrough. The irons gathering dust in your garage belong on a first tee. We want golfers trading directly with golfers — and trusting every step of it.

Secured payments

Checkout runs on Stripe, and every order is covered by buyer protection — if an item never arrives or isn't as described, open a dispute and we'll refund you in full.

6.5%

One flat seller fee

Sellers pay a flat 6.5% only when an item sells. No listing fees, no monthly charges, and no fees for buyers.

Golfers, verified

Sellers complete identity and payout verification before they can take a sale, so you know who you're buying from.

Direct between members

TeeBox is a venue — like the best parts of a pro-shop bulletin board, online. You set your price, you talk to the buyer, you make the deal. We just make it simple, safe, and built around the game you love.

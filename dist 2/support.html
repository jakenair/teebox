How do I delete my account?

Quick path inside the app:
  1. Sign in to TeeBox.
  2. Tap Account in the top nav (iPad/desktop) or the bottom nav (mobile).
  3. Tap the red Delete Account button — it appears at the top of your profile, directly below your name.
  4. Confirm in the dialog. Your account, listings, messages, and reviews are permanently removed.

If you'd rather request deletion by email, send a note from the address tied to your account to hello@teeboxmarket.com with the subject "Delete my account". We'll process it within 48 hours.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Active listings are removed immediately. Past order records are retained only as required for tax/regulatory compliance, with no personal identifiers.

Buying

How do I buy something?

  1. Browse the marketplace or search for what you want.
  2. Tap a listing to see details, photos, and the seller's profile.
  3. Tap Buy Now at the listed price, or Make Offer to negotiate.
  4. Pay securely via Stripe. Apple Pay, Google Pay, or any major card.

Is my payment safe?

All transactions run through Stripe. Your card details never touch our servers. Funds are held until the seller ships and you confirm receipt.

What if my item never arrives or isn't as described?

You have 7 days from delivery to Open a Dispute from the order page. We'll contact the seller and mediate a resolution. If the seller is at fault, you'll receive a full refund.

Selling

How do I sell on TeeBox?

  1. Sign in and tap the gold + Sell button.
  2. Verify your phone number and agree to the seller terms (one-time).
  3. Add photos, a title, brand, condition, and price.
  4. Publish. Buyers can message you or buy outright.

What's the seller fee?

Flat 6.5% on completed sales. No listing fees. No subscription. You pay only when something sells.

How do I get paid?

Payouts go directly to your bank via Stripe Connect, typically within 2 business days of the buyer confirming delivery.

Logo Bingo

What is Logo Bingo?

A daily golf-course identification game. Same 9 course logos worldwide each day, refreshing at midnight US Eastern. You get one guess per logo — guess correctly for points (faster = more), or the answer is revealed.

How is my score calculated?

100 points for a correct guess, plus a speed bonus: +200 if you answer in under 5 seconds, +100 under 10s, +50 under 20s, +20 under 30s. Wrong or revealed tiles score 0.

Can I share my results?

Yes — after the game ends, tap Share for an emoji-grid summary you can send to friends. Streaks are tracked locally on your device.

Seller Terms of Service

1. Seller Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old to sell on TeeBox. By agreeing to these terms you confirm that you are legally allowed to sell the items you list and that you have full ownership or authority to sell them.

2. Item Authenticity

All items listed on TeeBox must be genuine and accurately described. Listing counterfeit, replica, or misrepresented golf equipment is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and potential legal action. Every item sold through TeeBox is subject to our authentication process.

3. Accurate Listings

You agree to provide accurate descriptions, correct condition ratings, and honest photos of all items you list. Misrepresenting an item's condition, brand, model, or specifications is grounds for removal and suspension.

4. Shipping Obligations

Once a sale is confirmed, you agree to ship the item to TeeBox's authentication facility within 2 business days using the provided prepaid shipping label. Failure to ship on time may result in order cancellation and seller penalties.

5. Fees

TeeBox charges a seller transaction fee on each completed sale. The current fee structure is displayed in your seller dashboard. Fees are deducted automatically from your payout. TeeBox reserves the right to adjust fees with 30 days notice.

6. Payouts

Seller payouts are issued within 3 business days after the buyer receives and the item passes authentication. Payouts are sent via direct bank transfer. You are responsible for providing accurate banking information.

7. Prohibited Items

The following are not permitted on TeeBox: stolen goods, items under legal dispute, items with removed or altered serial numbers, and any non-golf related merchandise.

8. Account Suspension

TeeBox reserves the right to suspend or terminate seller accounts for violations of these terms, fraudulent activity, excessive cancellations, or poor seller performance metrics.

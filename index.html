About TeeBox

The peer-to-peer marketplace for golfers.

TeeBox is a place to buy and sell golf gear directly between members. We focus on the categories you actually care about — clubs from the top brands, premium apparel from labels like Peter Millar and Holderness & Bourne, accessories from Smathers & Branson, and the occasional piece of historic memorabilia.

How it works

  1. List in seconds. Verify your phone number, agree to the seller terms, and post photos with a price. No application, no waiting.
  2. Message before you buy. Every listing has a built-in chat with the seller so you can ask about condition, sizing, or shipping before you commit.
  3. Pay with confidence. Checkout runs through Stripe with Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Card details never touch our servers.
  4. Sellers ship directly. Once a sale is confirmed, the seller has 3 business days to ship with tracking. Funds are held until the buyer confirms delivery.
  5. Low fee. Sellers pay 6.5% on completed sales — no listing fees, no buyer fees.

What TeeBox is not

TeeBox is a venue, like eBay or Craigslist. We do not own the items, do not authenticate them, and we are not party to the transaction between buyer and seller. Read the Terms of Service for the full picture before you list or purchase.

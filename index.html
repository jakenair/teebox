All items listed on TeeBox must be genuine and accurately described. Listing counterfeit, replica, or misrepresented golf equipment is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and potential legal action.

3. Accurate Listings & Photos

You agree to provide accurate descriptions, correct condition ratings, and honest photos of all items you list. Photos must show the actual item being sold. Stock photos or photos of different items are not permitted.

Once a sale is confirmed, you agree to ship the item to TeeBox's authentication facility within 2 business days using the provided prepaid shipping label.

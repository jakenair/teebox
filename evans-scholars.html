The foundation behind the cause

TeeBox proudly stands behind the Western Golf Association and its Evans Scholars Foundation — the institution that has sent caddies to college on full scholarships since 1930.

Who the Evans Scholars are

The Evans Scholars Foundation — run by the Western Golf Association — awards full tuition-and-housing college scholarships to golf caddies with strong academics and real financial need. It started in 1930 with a single golfer's belief that the kids carrying our bags deserved a shot at a degree.

Nearly a century later, that belief has put more than 11,550 caddies through college. The Foundation is funded by 30,000-plus everyday supporters through its "Par Club," and by proceeds from the BMW Championship, a PGA TOUR playoff event.

Independently rated: Charity Navigator 4-star · Candid Platinum Seal of Transparency.

Why it matters

A loop around eighteen holes can become four years of college. For a caddie with the grades and the grit but not the means, an Evans Scholarship changes the whole arc of a life — and it's funded by people who simply love the game.
